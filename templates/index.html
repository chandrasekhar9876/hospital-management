Website Guide

How This Website Works

MediCore is role-based. After login, each user sees only the options they are allowed to use.

Patient Options

  • My Visits: Book appointment by selecting doctor, date, time, and issue.
  • Patient Portal: View EHR, lab reports, payments, and telemedicine links.
  • Medical Chatbot: Ask symptom guidance and then book quickly.

Doctor Options

  • My Appointments: See which patient is coming on which date/time and update status.
  • Doctor Tools: Add EHR, lab results, pharmacy stock, and telemedicine sessions.
  • Schedule View: Check upcoming patient list with issue details.

Admin Options

  • Dashboard: Monitor all appointments, doctors, patients, and statuses.
  • Operations: Manage billing, inventory, pharmacy alerts, and staff shifts.
  • Full Schedule: Track doctor schedule and patient appointment schedule in one place.

System Rules

  • Only patients can create appointments and submit payments.
  • Only assigned doctors can update their appointments and clinical notes.
  • Admin can see all schedules, billing entries, inventory, and staff rotation.
  • Appointment booking blocks already occupied doctor slots.
  • Patient payment entries are auto-synced into admin billing records.
